TrafficSight™ Tiered Pricing Model

Xjera Labs believes that quality data are vital for producing quality results. As such, TrafficSightTM offers a tiered pricing plan that allow users to process multiple video footages from an individual junction to facilitate the collection of highly accurate traffic data.
This enables users to achieve competitive price rates where the average price per movement decreases as they process more movements at a single intersection.

Mileage Rewards Scheme

By subscribing to TrafficSight™, you gain access to our mileage reward system where you can earn monthly rebate tokens from your uploads and processing orders.

All new users start off at Rank E and move up the ranking as more videos are processed through TrafficSight™. Ranking are based on the total amount of footage processed within a 12 months period and are updated monthly before the distribution of rebate tokens.

Our Clients



TrafficSight™

TrafficSight™ takes advantage of our award-winning algorithm in artificial intelligent video analytics to provide accurate traffic data with an accuracy above 90%* even in challenging condition, such as reflection shadows, shaky solution and night condition.


* Achieving a consistent performance above 90%. Performance of 70ms in response time with over 90% accuracy based on testing with NVIDIA advanced GPU

Traffic survey conducted through video analytics eradicates human error during the data collection process especially for long hour studies and remove any safety concern when human counters are deployed on-site for manual count survey. This provide you with a peace of mind when surveys are conducted.

Benefits of Traffic Data Collection with Video Analytics

The use of video analytics also allow higher resolution of data to be collected at a faster time, allowing for time saving in your projects.
